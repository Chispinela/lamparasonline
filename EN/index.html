How to find your ideal lamp?

Many people believe that any model or type of lamp works, but it is not. When a person looks for a lamp or any type of utensil, he looks mainly at aesthetics and price, but to correctly choose a lamp, several factors must be taken into account:

Compatibility with light bulbs: For a light bulb or LED to be mounted with a type of lamp, both have to be compatible, since if not the thread, that is, the lower part of the bulb would not fit with the lamp.

The size of the lamp: It is important to know the size of the lamp, since depending on it a lamp could be too cluttering, and on some websites if you do not look correctly at the size they could give you one much smaller than the one you want or on the contrary.

The type of bulb or led: When you have to choose a lamp, you not only have to look at the lamp, but also at the bulbs or LEDs, since they can have many functionalities or can even be controlled from your mobile.

Bulb Types: Pros and Cons

There are two main types of light bulbs:

Leds:

LEDs are the new light bulb, since they are like a normal light bulb except for three large variants:

They consume less energy: LEDs provide 90% of the light with respect to the energy they consume.

Lifespan: LEDs do not have filaments inside so they can last 50,000 hours without melting.

Technological design: LEDs turn on and off instantly and if a bluetooth device is incorporated they can be controlled from mobile devices.

We cannot forget the price, a led bulb costs on average 2 euros more than a filament bulb.
